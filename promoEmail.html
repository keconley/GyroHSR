Finishing beautiful
bathrooms starts with
these tips and techniques.
Bathrooms pose unique challenges and are usually only as good as what lies beneath the paint and tile. Which is why USG is providing you with the tips and insight to always choose the right backerboard or underlayment
for the job.

Watch as we make choosing the right substrate a
breeze. Here’s what you will discover by watching
the video.
.  
Casement Board—It’s the answer for wet areas like bathroom floors, walls and showers. DUROCK® cement board Next Gen provides superior tile bond with natural mold-resistance.  
   
.  
Alternative Backerboard—Unlike cement
board, alternative backerboard like FIBEROCK®
Tile Backerboard can be finished with ceramic
or stone tile, paint or wallpaper.
   
.  
Tile Membrane—Available on a roll, it’s
lightweight and easy to install over a variety of
interior surfaces. DUROCK™ Tile Membrane can
be used on a number of surfaces including
floors, walls and countertops. It can even be
used over existing ceramic tile and vinyl.
