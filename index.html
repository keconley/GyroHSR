Real Time RTI Tracking,
Real Financial Benefits.
Lost RTIs Can Cost You a Lot of Money
For many companies, Returnable Transport Items
(RTIs) represent a substantial capital investment.
Unfortunately, these units are often damaged,
stockpiled, cycled inefficiently and experience
shrinkage that can reach up to 25% annually.
A Simple Solution That Can Pay for Itself
Motorola’s Radio Frequency Identification (RFID)
is an affordable and efficient way to track individual
RTI assets in real time. Our RFID system allows you
to increase visibility, allocation and accountability
while reducing shrinkage which, in turn, will help
you reduce your operating costs, decrease your RTI
inventory and lower your total cost of ownership.
In fact, the benefits of implementing an RFID
tracking system are so significant, it can quickly
pay for itself.
 
Experience That Counts
